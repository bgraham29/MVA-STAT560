\begin{equation*}
P \overset{set}{=} \frac{\rho^2}{1 - \rho^2}
\end{equation*}
To find the eigenvalues and eigenvectors, solve
\begin{eqnarray*}
\begin{bmatrix}
P - \lambda & P \\
P & P - \lambda
\end{bmatrix} & = & \mathbf{0} \\
(P - \lambda)^2 - P^2 & = & 0\\
P^2 - 2P\lambda + \lambda^2 - P^2 & = & 0 \\
\lambda^2 - 2P\lambda & = & 0 \\
\lambda^2 & = & 2P\lambda \\
\lambda & = & 2P = \frac{\rho^2}{1 - \rho^2}
\end{eqnarray*}
